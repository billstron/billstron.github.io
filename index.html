William J. Burke
William J. Burke is the Lead Algorithm Engineer for the GE Nucleus home energy manager. I currently work in Louisville Kentucky at GE Appliance Park, but I recently (May 2010) finished my PhD in Mechanical Engineering at the University of California Berkeley. My adviser was David Auslander, and my research focused on the design and control of large scale distributed energy systems. Further, I'm a husband and father living in the Highland neighborhood of Louisville.
